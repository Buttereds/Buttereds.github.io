About Project

Creating safe, supportive, and inclusive environments, this immersive VR training highlights the diverse perspectives that exist in any situation, enabling learners to develop more thoughtful responses. The Diversity, Inclusion, and Belonging (DIB) module allows learners to approach the topic in a safe, immersive environment. It features an emotionally charged scenario that encourages participants to explore and reflect on their reactions, challenging them to recognize and address any biases.
