03 VR SIMULATION — PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT

COACHING
CONVERSATIONS

AI-driven VR coaching simulator — practise difficult workplace conversations with a character that responds to what you actually say.

OVERVIEW

Coaching Conversations puts learners face-to-face with a photorealistic AI character in a sleek, purpose-built VR environment. The simulation is designed for managers and team leads who need to practise high-stakes conversations — delivering critical feedback, handling conflict, navigating performance reviews — in a safe, repeatable space.

Unlike scripted training videos, the AI character responds dynamically to what the learner says and how they say it.

MY ROLE

Lead programmer. I built the full dialogue engine, NPC animation state machine, speech recognition integration, and the post-session analytics and scoring system including real-time lip sync driven by live audio.

TECHNICAL HIGHLIGHTS

  • Real-time lip sync via audio phoneme analysis driving blend-shape facial animation
  • Speech recognition integration (Azure Cognitive Services) for live voice input
  • Branching dialogue engine with sentiment weighting — character "mood" shifts based on conversation quality
  • Session analytics: tracks response quality, pacing, keyword usage, and sentiment over time
  • Custom VR environment with dynamic lighting to shift emotional tone across conversation stages
