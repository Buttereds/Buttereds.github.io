OVERVIEW

HexBreak is a mobile puzzle game centred on a custom hexagonal grid physics system. Players fire tiles into a hex field, triggering chain reactions to clear the board within a move limit. Simple to learn, deep to master.

The game launched to immediate chart success — ranked #3 in puzzle games on launch day and featured by Apple in "Games We Love" within the first week.

MY ROLE

Lead developer on a two-person team. I built the entire physics and game logic layer, the procedural level generator, the monetisation systems, and handled the iOS and Android submission pipeline. My partner handled art and audio.

TECHNICAL HIGHLIGHTS

  • Custom hex-grid physics with neighbour-chaining and cascade scoring
  • Procedural level generation with difficulty curve modelling
  • Optimised for 60fps on devices 5 years old — zero Unity physics, fully custom
  • IAP and ad integration with privacy-first consent flow (GDPR/CCPA compliant)
  • Dual platform submission pipeline using Unity Cloud Build
