OVERVIEW

Teamwork is a 2D side-scrolling platformer built as a serious game for teamwork skills training. Players control a team of three characters — each with a distinct ability — and must switch between them to overcome obstacles that no single character can solve alone.

The core design principle: the mechanic is the message. Players naturally internalise that different strengths are complementary — not through instruction, but through the experience of failing without collaboration and succeeding with it.

MY ROLE

Lead developer. I built the character switching system, all platformer physics, the level scripting pipeline, and the scoring and debrief system.

TECHNICAL HIGHLIGHTS

  • Three-character switching system with per-character ability states and seamless control handoff
  • Custom 2D platformer physics — fully kinematic for precise, predictable feel
  • Level scripting system allowing non-developers to author challenge sequences via data assets
  • Adaptive difficulty — tracks player performance and adjusts obstacle density accordingly
  • Debrief screen with per-decision analysis mapped to teamwork learning outcomes
