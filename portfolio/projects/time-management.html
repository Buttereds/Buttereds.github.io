OVERVIEW

Time Management is a 2D serious game designed to teach time management and prioritisation skills. Players are presented with a list of daily activities — each with a time cost — and must drag them into a structured day plan before a countdown timer expires.

Once planned, the game shifts to a top-down view where the player "lives" their scheduled day in real time, seeing the consequences of good and poor planning play out directly.

MY ROLE

Lead developer. I designed and built the drag-and-drop scheduling system, the real-time day simulation, the consequence and scoring logic, and the top-down character controller.

TECHNICAL HIGHLIGHTS

  • Drag-and-drop schedule builder with time-slot collision detection and visual feedback
  • Real-time simulation layer — day progresses on a scaled clock, triggering activity events in sequence
  • Consequence system — missed or rushed activities have visible in-game effects
  • Top-down character controller with room-to-room navigation driven by the planned schedule
  • Post-session debrief scoring aligned to time management learning objectives
