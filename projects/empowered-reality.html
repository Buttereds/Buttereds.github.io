OVERVIEW

Empowered Reality is the branded VR gateway experience for the SkillsVR platform. Before learners begin any training module, they are immersed in a purpose-built environment that introduces them to VR interaction, spatial navigation, and the SkillsVR product identity.

The experience was designed to reduce learner anxiety around VR headsets — particularly for enterprise audiences encountering the technology for the first time — while reinforcing brand identity through environmental storytelling.

MY ROLE

Lead developer. Responsible for all Unity implementation including environment setup, interaction systems, spatial audio integration, and performance optimisation for standalone Meta Quest delivery.

TECHNICAL HIGHLIGHTS

  • Built on Unity XR Interaction Toolkit — fully compatible with Meta Quest 2, 3, and Pro
  • Custom spatial audio rig using FMOD for immersive ambient design
  • Optimised to 72fps on standalone Quest hardware with zero frame drops
  • Dynamic LOD system and occlusion culling tuned for the environment scale
  • Integrated with the SkillsVR platform launcher — seamless session handoff
