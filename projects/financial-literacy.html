08 2D GAME — FINANCIAL LITERACY

FINANCIAL
LITERACY

Run Toni's Taco Truck from the ground up — teaching budgeting, pricing, staffing, and profit/loss through play.

OVERVIEW

Financial Literacy is a business simulation built around running Toni's Taco Truck. Players choose a location, set menu prices, manage ingredient costs, hire and pay staff, and serve customers across multiple trading rounds — with every decision feeding into a real profit and loss statement.

Abstract concepts like unit economics, staffing ratios, and margin become immediately tangible when you can see exactly why your taco truck is losing money this round.

MY ROLE

Lead developer. I built the full simulation engine — the financial model, staffing and inventory systems, customer demand simulation, and the round-by-round P&L reporting.

TECHNICAL HIGHLIGHTS

  • Financial simulation engine — models revenue, COGS, staffing costs, and net profit per trading round
  • Dynamic demand model — customer footfall varies by location, time of day, and stock levels
  • Staffing allocation system — hire, pay, and assign staff with trade-offs between cost and service capacity
  • Inventory management with restock decisions and wastage mechanics
  • End-of-round P&L breakdown screen for debrief and reflection
